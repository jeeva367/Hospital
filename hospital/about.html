Best Medical & Health care Needs to Our Patients

MY Hospitals Enterprise Limited is an Indian multinational hospital chain headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded by Jeeva M in 2024 as the first corporate healthcare provider in India. Several of the MY's hospitals have been among the first in India to receive international healthcare accreditation by the America-based Joint Commission International (JCI) as well as 13 NABH National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers hospitals.

In addition to providing hospitals, MY's has developed services in telemedicine, after starting a pilot project in 2030 at Aragonda, vijay's home village. MY's signed an MoU with Health Education England in April 2034 to provide a large number of doctors to fill vacancies in the English National Health Service.
